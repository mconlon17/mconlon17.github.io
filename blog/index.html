General musings on the community, the software, the data, the semantic web, applications, data management, institutional and scholarly use, updates, and whatever else comes to mind.

Getting Started Posted February 8, 2014

I've never done a blog before, so please bear with me. Seems like there's a lot to share about happenings in the VIVO community, tools development and data management.

I'm particularly interested in data. For VIVO, it's usually data about scholarship -- representing scholarly activity in all its forms. At the University of Florida (UF for short), we have started with basic data -- people and their departments, grants and their investigators, papers and their authors, courses and their instructors.

Here I'll be writing (blogging?) about what we've done, the tools we've created, and tools we are creating to make use of the data in VIVO. I'll point at the repos of the software.

I hope you find something useful
